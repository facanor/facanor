Engineer, researcher and amateur gamer

I am passionate about technology and computing, that's why I decided to study Systems Engineering, and I did it at the Universidad de los Llanos . I learned to apply my knowledge in other areas and this sparked my interest in cancer research.

I decided to study a master's degree in this area. I did my graduate studies in Biomedical Engineering at the Universidad Nacional de Colombia , exploring different topics in digital pathology.

This immersion was not enough, which is why I am studying for a doctorate, in the face of new ideas and interesting problems. There is a long road ahead.

When I'm not studying, I like to cook, try different recipes, play video games and spend time with my family and friends.
